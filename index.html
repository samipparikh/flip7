How to Play

Overview

Flip cards one at a time. If you flip a number you already have — you BUST and score nothing for the round!

The Deck (94 cards)

Number cards: one 1, two 2s, three 3s, four 4s, five 5s, six 6s, seven 7s. Plus special cards mixed in.

On Your Turn

  • FLIP — Reveal the next card from the deck
  • STOP — Bank your current points and end your turn

Busting

If you flip a number card that matches a number you already have face-up, you BUST! You score 0 points this round.

Scoring

  • Your score = sum of all your face-up number cards
  • 7-Card Bonus: If you reach exactly 7 cards without busting, your score is DOUBLED!

Special Cards

  • +2 Bonus — Adds 2 extra points to your score
  • +4 Bonus — Adds 4 extra points to your score
  • Second Chance — Saves you from one bust (discards the duplicate)
  • Freeze — Forces another player to stop (they bank their current points)

Winning

Play 3 rounds. Highest total score wins!
